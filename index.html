Welcome to The Feral Truth

This website explores feral hogs, detailing their origins, rapid reproduction, and the significant environmental, agricultural, and economic damage they cause to ecosystems and communities. It emphasizes the importance of regulated hog hunting as a management tool, showcasing various methods while highlighting safety and ethical practices. Incorporating personal experiences from living in an affected area, the site illustrates the profound impact of feral hogs on land and ecosystems, framing hog hunting as a vital responsibility rather than merely a pastime. By addressing common myths and misconceptions, the website aims to raise awareness about the urgent need for management and our shared duty to protect natural resources.
