Welcome to The Feral Truth

This website explores the growing issue of feral hogs by explaining what they are, where they originated, and why their rapid reproduction and behavior make them a serious invasive species. It examines the extensive environmental, agricultural, and economic damage caused by feral hogs, emphasizing the widespread impact on ecosystems, farmland, and communities. The site also discusses why regulated hog hunting is an important population management tool that helps protect natural resources and reduce destruction. In addition, it provides an overview of common hog hunting and control methods while stressing the importance of safety, legality, and ethical practices. Finally, the website addresses common myths and misconceptions about feral hogs and hog hunting, using factual information to promote a better understanding of the issue.
